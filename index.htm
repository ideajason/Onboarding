Mandatory Procedures

The following steps must be completed for every New Hire regardless of their position and if they are an employee of OCCM or a contractor.

No. Task POC/Comments Completed
1. Submit a requisition for a New Hire in Cornerstone and notify IT, HR, and Finance Contact and notify Antoine Alexander(IT), Tom Schaeffer(HR), and Tim Goodman(Finance).
2. Notify the front desk when you schedule an in-person interview. Contact Sabrina Clark, this is so she is aware you are expecting a visitor to sign-in at the front desk.

Note: You only need to notify Sabrina about in person interviews.

3. Contact IT, HR, Finance, and the Front Desk once you establish the start date for the New Hire Contact Antoine Alexander(IT), Tom Schaeffer (HR), Tim Goodman (Finance), and Sabrina Clark (Front Desk).
4. Request a PC, Desk Phone, and OCCM Domain Account Use this link to access the form click here Or you can reach the form manually. Follow this path; OneIT Homepage > Service Catalogue >OnBoarding Services > Information Technology
5. Setup New Hire in Innotas Fill out the required form, once complete send the email but cc Scott Saxon.
6. Add New Hire to the Polaris_Project and Polaris_IT Team email list Contact Brent Bodiford (brent_bodiford@onecallcm.com) to add the New Hire to the Polaris_Project distribution list and to add the New Hire to the Polaris IT Team distribution list
7. Add New Hire to the Polaris IT Org Chart Data POC/Comments
8. Request Sharepoint Access Contact Jason Williams. Either yourself or the New Hire can provide Jason his/her full name and the level of access that is required. Contact Jason within the New Hire’s first week at work
9. Request TFS Access POC/Comments

Employee Self-Service
New hires can enter their own information into the onboarding software before their first day.

Situational Procedures

The following procedures are contingent on the type of hire and may not always need to be completed. Please check and enter date completed.

No. Task Contact/Comments Situation Completed
1. Review Cornerstone Reach out to the New Hire and ask if HR contacted them about having their time sheet set up. HR usually has this set this up about three days into their first week.
2. Fill out Off Boarding Form Review Cornerstone to ensure the New Hire is set up correctly as a part of your team.
3. Ensure desk is clean Ensure desk is clean prior to New Hire’s start date.
4. Set up printer Ensure the appropriate print drivers are installed and that the New Hire can print (Print Service Setup). Try to do this prior to the New Hire’s first day.
5. OCCM Orientation Ensure the OCCM Orientation is scheduled
6. Employee Expectations Review Employee Expectations
7. Self-Service Set up Share with the New Hire the OCCM Self-Service Document. This is for unlocking their account and resetting their password.
8. Provide Starter Supplies Contact Sabrina Clark at the front desk with a list of starter supplies the New Hire will need. This does not include technical hardware.

Offboarding Procedures

No. Task Contact/Comments Completed
1. Contact HR Jacksonville, FL Baymeadows contact Jessica Siefert and Christy Turner for Downtown. If individual is FTE provide Jessica or Christy the resignation letter. If it is a contractor notify HR asap when you expect them to be off boarded. Let HR know if they are eligible for re-hire.
2. Fill out Off Boarding Form Navigate to Service-Now and submit a completed off-boarding form. The form can be accessed here. Note: You must be logged into ServiceNow
3. Return HardWare This includes mobile phones, conferencing equipment, laptops, computer equipment, and any equipment owned by OCCM. Return to the on-sight Help Desk crew. Baymeadows = Antoine Alexander Downtown =
4. Return Badges Return ALL badges to Sabrina Clark (including an evening badge). If Sabrina is not available return the badge to Jessica Siefert.
5. Return Alarm Codes If your employee was given an individual alarm code for any of the suites you must notify Sabrina Clark and provide her the unique alarm code.
6. Remove from Distribution Lists Contact Brent Bodiford to have employee removed from all Polaris email distribution lists. If employee was part of IT leadership contact Scott Saxon to remove him or her from the leadership distribution list.
7. Remove PluralSight License Contact Brent Bodiford to remove any licenses associated to the employee. Usually only for Full Time Employees.
8. Remove SharePoint access Contact Jason Williams and ensure the employee is removed from access into the Polaris SharePoint site.
9. Remove VSTS access Contact John Downs and ensure the employee is removed from access to visual studio team services.
10. Remove from IT Org Chart Follow the link here. Remove the employee from your department’s tab. Please do not change the sheet labeled total.
